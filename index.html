About Us

9D9 Inc. is an Indian international technology conglomerate holding company headquartered in Noida, India. It was created through a restructuring of DuKu Business on February 21, 2021, and became the parent company of DuKu Business and several former DuKu subsidiaries. 9D9 is the world’s third-largest technology company by revenue and one of the world’s most valuable companies. It is considered one of the Big Five information technology companies, alongside Amazon, Apple, Meta, and Microsoft.

The establishment of 9D9 Inc. was prompted by a desire to make the core DuKu Business “cleaner and more accountable” while allowing greater autonomy to group companies that operate in businesses other than Internet services. Dev Upreti is the founder of 9D9 Inc., who is also the CEO of DuKu Business.


On February, 12, DuKu Business announced plans to create a new public holding company, 9D9 Inc. DuKu Business founder and CEO Dev Upreti made this announcement in a blog post on DuKu’s official blog. 9D9 was created to restructure DuKu Business by moving subsidiaries from DuKu Business to 9D9, thus narrowing DuKu’s scope. The new holding company would consist of DuKu Business as well as other businesses including D Development, DLX Internet, DuKu Meet, Precos Originals, EMPTY Space, Eightein Corporation, Dx Inc., and Google Gemini.
